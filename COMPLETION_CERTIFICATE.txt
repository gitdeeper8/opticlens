║                                    🔭 PROJECT OVERVIEW & SIGNIFICANCE                                           ║
╚════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╝

OPTICLENS introduces the first comprehensive computational framework for modeling the full spectrum of optical 
anomalies arising from the interaction of photons with aerosols, hydrometeors, and thermally stratified atmospheric 
layers. The framework integrates Mie scattering theory, Edlén refractive index equations, Rytov turbulence models, 
and Physics-Informed Neural Networks (PINNs) to create a unified description of atmospheric optical behavior.
